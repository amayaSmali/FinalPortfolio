Overview

    I took a Canadian Goverment specific algorithmic impact assessment and applied content strategy, prototyping and user testing to create a globally applicable streamlined questionnaire.


    My role was to conduct UX research, prototyping and UI design.

    Process

      I started with a excel sheet and created a lo-fi prototype with powerpoint slides, then a survey platform. I sent it to several colleges and asked followup questions. I observed their behavior while they took the assessment and timed them.


      As result I created a high-fidelity prototype of what the assessment could be like on the AI Global Website.


      Then I created a platform for where users can get their results from the assessment


      Objective and Wrap Up

      I designed the assessment this way to streamline a in depth assessment and reduce the time it takes users to complete the assessment and then get resources to help understand the results
